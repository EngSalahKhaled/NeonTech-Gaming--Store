About NeonTech

ESTABLISHED 2026 // VERSION 1.0

Welcome to the Next Generation of Gaming Gear

NeonTech was created with one single obsession: providing competitive gamers, creators, and hardware enthusiasts with state-of-the-art gaming equipment that matches their skills and lighting aspirations.

Founded by Salah Khaled, a dedicated product manager and hardware specialist, NeonTech merges ultra-premium components (such as hot-swappable mechanical keyboards, high-precision mice, elite VR controllers, and pro-level ergonomics) with stunning custom RGB atmospheres.

Our Core Values

  • Obsessive Engineering: We believe that details matter. From individual keycaps to mouse sensors, every item is carefully selected and tested.
  • Aesthetic Excellence: We don't do plain designs. Every product cataloged at NeonTech features signature custom glows, cyber styling, and harmony.
  • Customer-First Support: Support shouldn't be a hassle. Enjoy direct, live client assistance via Discord or WhatsApp 24/7.

The RGB Customization Philosophy

At NeonTech, customizability isn't just a marketing word—it's at the core of our website. Using our interactive RGB Customizer panel (available on the bottom-left), users can dynamically redefine our digital atmosphere. Shift from default Cyber Green to deep Arctic Cyan, custom Cyber Pink, or Glorious Gold to match your battle station directly.

Elite Partnerships

We collaborate directly with the world's most trusted gaming giants, including Razer, SteelSeries, Logitech G, Corsair, HyperX, and ASUS ROG, bringing exclusive drops and verified elite products to your desk.
