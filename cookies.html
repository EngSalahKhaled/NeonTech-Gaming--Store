Local Storage & Cookie Guidelines

To ensure NeonTech loads instantly, remains fully functional offline (via Service Workers), and preserves your gaming configuration, we utilize local browser memory capabilities instead of standard tracking cookies.

1. What We Store Locally

Instead of uninvited tracking code that follows you across the web, we store clean parameters directly inside your browser cache (`localStorage`):

  • `neontech-cart`: Keeps track of items added to your shopping cart.
  • `neontech-rgb-theme`: Remembers your active color preset customization (Green, Pink, Cyan, Gold).
  • `neontech_theme`: Remembers your base interface setting (Dark or Light UI mode).
  • `neontech_wishlist` / `neontech_comparison`: Keeps your liked products and comparison grids active.

2. Managing Your Settings

Because all this data is kept inside your browser, **you have absolute command over it**. Cleansing your browser history, deleting local site data, or running standard privacy extensions will immediately reset your shopping cart, preferences, and interface setups without leaving any residue.

3. Consenting to Settings

By continuing to navigate the NeonTech Gaming Store and customize your RGB profiles, you consent to our clean browser-level storage configuration. Enjoy a high-speed, secure, and privacy-respecting competitive gaming workspace!
