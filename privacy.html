How We Protect Your Gaming Data

At NeonTech, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you share with us. This policy details how we collect, store, and process your data across our entire client-side platform.

1. Information We Collect

We keep data collection minimal and focused strictly on delivering a seamless retail and gaming interface:

  • Account Data: If you register or log in, your secure credentials (email, hashed password, name) are authenticated and securely managed using our client-side database layer via **Supabase Auth**.
  • Shopping Cart & Preferences: Your active gear cart selections, liked products, and customized RGB mood atmosphere codes are stored directly inside your browser using secure local storage (`localStorage`). We do not store these details on external servers.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Signing up for drops stores your email securely in our newsletter index. If offline, the submission gracefully saves locally inside your browser cache before securely syncing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We utilize personal data strictly for operations:

  • Authenticating your user profile for tracking product reviews and orders.
  • Formatting invoice billing sheets and compiling precise WhatsApp-ready emoji messages for fast order dispatch.
  • Delivering pro-setup guides, discounts, and drops to subscribed member emails.

3. Data Retention & Control

Since we utilize browser-level storage (`localStorage`) for standard user actions, **you retain complete control of your data**. Cleansing your browser history/cache instantly purges all shopping cart listings, local preferences, and custom atmospheres from your browser memory.

4. Third-Party Services

Our operations are backed by verified third-party partners:

  • Supabase: For secure database user account auth.
  • WhatsApp wa.me API: Re-routing checkout text orders safely to the seller chat without intermediate server logs.
